## Key Structure Rules

- Every node starts with a **role** (`heading`, `paragraph`, `link`, `button`, `StaticText`, `cell`, `columnheader`, `row`, `listitem`, `list`, `listmarker`)
- Interactive elements have **[ref=eXX]** for click/type
- State info: `[expanded=true/false]`, `[level=N]`
- Text content lives in `StaticText "..."` — these are the actual readable words
- `code` / `strong` / `em` are separate roles — their text is also under StaticText
- The tree mirrors DOM nesting via indentation
